## 1. Project Purpose

This project is a **hardware design project** for a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) asset tracking tag called "IN100 BT TAG". 

**Primary Domain:** IoT / Asset Tracking Hardware

**Problem it solves:**
- Provides a compact BLE-based asset tracking/tagging solution
- The device appears to be a CR2032 coin-cell powered Bluetooth beacon/tag
- Used for tracking assets or items via Bluetooth Low Energy technology
- The technical requirements PDF suggests this is a commercial/industrial product with defined specifications
